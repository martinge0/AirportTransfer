Services

Transport services

Our company specializes in transporting passengers to and from Bourgas or other Europe airports as well as the airports of Istanbul. We offer you experienced drivers who will transport you safely to a specified location given by you. Please be assured that you will always arrive on time, without the stress of parking, relaxed and happy. You make us familiar only with the number of your flight and everything else will be left upon us.

24/7 service

Transfer services are available 24 /7 to private address, hotel, bus-stop, railway station or other places. Our employees and professional team of drivers are available 24/7. We accept orders 24 hours a day.

Easy to connect

Appropriate for you services. Your driver will meet you holding board – sign with your name on it in the arrivals hall and will help you with the luggage. If there is flight delay, we will be informed and will meet you on the hour of arrival.

Low prices

Our prices are final and include all expenses such as road fees and fuel. There aren’t hidden fees. All prices published on the web page are valid for the day time transfers as well as the night transfers. We work WITHOUT ANY MADIATORS, therefore we will offer best price for you. You should place your order not later than 12 hours prior the flight. Do not forget to let us know the number of the travellers in order to send the suitable vehicle.
